THE VIRUS
This is an ongoing comic of mine which I started in 2020 back during the pandemic days. It is actually a spin-off of The Computer, which is a series that can be found in the videos section. It was originally going to be a 30-minute animated short film on my channel, but I found that it was incredibly difficult and taxing for me to produce that long of an animation, which was already massively shortened as is. I decided against it and went with the comic you see today, which currently has 4 episodes.
THE VIRUS EP.1 - PROLOGUE: AN UNEXPECTED FINDING
CJ finds a mysterious file in his laptop's recycling bin.
THE VIRUS EP.2 - ORIGINS OF THE VIRUS PT.1
An expert hacker demonstrates his creation's dangerous abilities.
THE VIRUS EP.3 - ORIGINS OF THE VIRUS PT.2
The virus decides their next course of action while trapped in a box.
THE VIRUS EP.4 - INFECTIOUS CIVILIZATION PT.1
The virus enters a secret civilization of retired computer viruses.
