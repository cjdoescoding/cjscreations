FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S: GOLDEN TEARS

Genre: Drama, Horror

Synopsis: Two years after losing their youngest in the Bite of '83, the Afton family remains ravaged by grief and guilt. As they reach their breaking point, the vengeful spirit of Golden Freddy reawakens - eager to hunt them down. Siblings Michael and Elizabeth Afton are forced to go on the run, desperate for safety - and answers.
